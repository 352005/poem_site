HAYZEE'S
POETRY

Poetry, a whispered word,
A rhyme, a line, a verse,
A heart that sings, a tale that's heard,
A universe, in a hearse.

A word that paints, a thought that flows,
A feeling that we share,
A journey that nobody knows,
A road that takes us there.

Poetry, a simple thing,
A way to express, to be,
A way to spread our wings,
And let our souls free.

About Me

My name is Abdulazeez Ibrahim. I'm a poet from Kogi state, Nigeria.

I developed interest in poetry right from a young age and I started writing them while I was in junior secondary school.

To me, poetry was a way to express myself, to show how my feelings, to express the love I had for nature and life itself.

I started learning more about poetry, poems and famous poets like William Shakespeare, Chinua Achebe, Robert Frost, Wole Soyinka and the likes. My favorite being Wole Soyinka, I really learnt alot from his journey in poetry.
